What is PRODUCE?

Building on the legacy of the previous LGBTIQ+ Screens project, PRODUCE extends the research into LGBTIQ+ audiovisual content production. This interdisciplinary project examines how production contexts, institutional frameworks, and professional practices shape LGBTIQ+ representation in Spanish audiovisual media.

What do we do?

PRODUCE investigates the production dynamics of LGBTIQ+ content, understanding how production contexts and intersectional variables such as age, gender, ethnicity, and class of producers influence content creation. We analyze narrative strategies, representation patterns, and the role of streaming platforms in shaping LGBTIQ+ audiovisual narratives.

Expected Outcomes

PRODUCE aims at mapping how production dynamics shape LGBTIQ+ audiovisual content in the Spanish state. Our research will be disseminated through peer reviewed publications, training programs for communication students, and collaborative initiatives with industry stakeholders to foster inclusive production practices.

About us

PRODUCE is a coordinated effort made possible by the active participation of researchers from universities and institutions across Spain and internationally. We bring together expertise in media studies, communication, audiovisual production, and critical LGBTIQ+ studies.

The project is led by the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona, in collaboration with international partners.
